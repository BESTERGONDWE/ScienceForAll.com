Preventing antimicrobial resistance requires a multi-pronged approach:

• Use antibiotics only when they are truly needed. If you don't need an antibiotic, don't take one.
• Use antibiotics correctly. Take the full course of the antibiotic as prescribed, even if you start to feel better.
• Practice good hygiene. This includes hand washing and disinfecting surfaces.

Healthcare professionals can also help prevent antimicrobial resistance by:
• Prescribing antibiotics only when they are needed.
• Choosing the right antibiotic for the infection.
